# shortlist-decision-brief

Use this skill to finalize tool decisions with a clear pilot path.

## Required Inputs

- scored shortlist
- risk register
- budget and contract constraints
- deployment timeline target

## Execution Sequence

1. Select top two to three options from weighted score.
2. Validate no critical unmitigated risk remains.
3. Draft decision memo with:
   - recommendation
   - alternatives considered
   - rejection rationale
4. Define pilot success metrics and stop criteria.
5. Assign decision owner and review checkpoint.

## Output Contract

Return:

- decision_recommendation
- alternatives_table
- pilot_plan_30_day
- success_metrics
- stop_or_scale criteria

## Failure Conditions

- recommendation without tradeoff analysis
- no stop criteria for pilot
- no explicit owner
